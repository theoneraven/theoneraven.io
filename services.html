Your answers come from Within

Humans have been hunting the truth for thousands of years, but there are almost as many paths to the one truth. Digging through all the knowledge out there and how to even begin is the utmost of daunting tasks. Depending on your situation, we can develop the best way for you to gain insight into why you are here, how to move forward in life, why you may be stuck somehow, and even who you really are. I can offer my own gifts to help, but ultimately, you need a plan that you can stick with for the rest of your life... so you can live your best life.

Readings, or Not

I'm not a "psychic" and don't do "readings"- in the popular sense. However, I will use certain methods (journeying, remote viewing, even tarot) if I feel that it is necessary for your individual situation. I am able to "see" auras and energy, and am adept at energy healing. I am highly tuned to nature and other dimensions, and when possible, prefer to work outside.

Payment

If one is good at something, one should never do it for free. With that being said, I don't charge a flat rate for any of my services. I feel that you should tell me what you are willing to pay or give in return for what I can give you. Cash, food, raw materials, etc. There is always an opportunity for negotiation and I do believe in bartering.
